Section 2: Attention & Focus (9 Questions)

Rate how often these occur in your daily life

Q1: Do you often fail to give close attention to details, or make careless mistakes in your work, schoolwork, or during other activities?

Examples: Not reading instructions carefully, working too quickly and making mistakes, missing important details

Q2: Do you often find it difficult to sustain your attention during tasks or activities?

Examples: Can't keep attention on tasks for long, quickly distracted by your own thoughts, hard to watch a film or read a book to the end

Q3: Does it often seem as though you are not listening when someone speaks to you directly?

Examples: Dreamy or preoccupied, difficulty concentrating on conversations, not knowing what was said afterward

Q4: Do you often fail to follow through on instructions and fail to finish tasks or meet obligations?

Examples: Start things but don't complete them, difficulty completing tasks once novelty wears off, need deadlines

Q5: Do you often find it difficult to organize tasks and activities?

Examples: Difficulty planning daily life, disorganized space, arriving late, poor time management, double-booking

Q6: Do you often avoid or have an aversion to tasks that require sustained mental effort?

Examples: Postpone boring or difficult tasks, do easy things first, avoid paperwork or reading

Q7: Do you often lose things that are necessary for tasks or activities?

Examples: Misplace wallet/keys, lose papers, spend time searching for things, leave things behind

Q8: Are you often easily distracted by external stimuli or unrelated thoughts?

Examples: Easily distracted by noises/events, difficulty filtering information, hard to pick up the thread again

Q9: Are you often forgetful in daily activities?

Examples: Forget appointments, need frequent reminders, return home for forgotten things, forget to check agenda

Section 3: Hyperactivity & Restlessness (9 Questions)

Rate how often these occur in your daily life

Q10: Do you often fidget with your hands or feet, or squirm in your seat?

Examples: Difficulty sitting still, tapping pen, playing with things, fiddling with hair, biting nails

Q11: Do you often stand up in situations where remaining seated is expected?

Examples: Avoid long meetings/lectures, prefer walking around, never sit still for long, make excuses to move

Q12: Do you often feel restless or have difficulty relaxing?

Examples: Feel restless inside, constantly need to be doing something, hard to relax, feel agitated

Q13: Do you often have difficulty engaging in leisure activities quietly?

Examples: Being loud in various situations, talking during activities, difficulty doing things quietly

Q14: Are you often "on the go" or act as if "driven by a motor"?

Examples: Always busy doing something, too much energy, constantly on the move, excessively driven

Q15: Do you often talk excessively?

Examples: Known as talkative, hard to stop talking, tendency to talk too much, not letting others speak

Q16: Do you often blurt out answers before questions have been completed?

Examples: Saying what you think immediately, giving answers before others finish speaking, completing others' words

Q17: Do you often have difficulty waiting your turn?

Examples: Difficulty waiting in queues, impatient in traffic, can't wait turn in conversations, being impatient

Q18: Do you often interrupt or intrude on others?

Examples: Quick to interfere, interrupts others, disturbs activities without being asked, has opinions on everything
